# Tax Rate Sources

All tax rates and rules are verified against official Lithuanian government sources.

## GPM (Gyventojų pajamų mokestis)
- **VMI GPM tarifai**: https://www.vmi.lt/evmi/5725
- **2026 progresinis GPM**: https://www.vmi.lt/evmi/gyventoju-pajamu-mokescio-pakeitimai-nuo-2026-m.
  - Civil contract (code 77): progressive 15%/20%/25%/32% based on VDU thresholds
  - Dividends: flat 15%

## Sodra — MB narys
- **MB nario įmokos**: https://sodra.lt/imokos/esu-mazosios-bendrijos-narys
  - Civilinė sutartis (code 77): VSD 0%, PSD 0%
  - Lėšos asmeniniams poreikiams (code 02): VSD 13.83%, PSD 6.98%
  - Bazė code 02: 50% (iki 2026-06-30), 90% (nuo 2026-07-01)
- **Sodra tarifai 2026**: https://sodra.lt/sodros-imoku-tarifai-taikomi-nuo-2026-m-sausio-1-d-savarankiskai-dirbantiems-asmenims
- **Sodra lubos**: 43 VDU savarankiškai dirbantiems
